Project Summary

Water, once an abundant natural resource, is becoming a more valuable commodity due to droughts and overuse. That's why Water Management is important.
It's a Raspberry Pi based project that monitors the amount of water consumed by Households.
The tentative plan is to display the consumption details on Django Based Web Portal.
